How the data works

Straight talk about where the numbers come from, so you never make a real cellar decision on a made-up figure.

  • The wines: search runs on a built-in list of notable producers, and expands to the full LWIN reference catalogue (100k+ wines) when a wines.json file sits next to this page. Either way, you can add any bottle manually.
  • Critic scores & market value: you enter these yourself. There's no free public feed for live Wine Spectator / Wine Advocate / Vinous scores or Wine-Searcher / Liv-ex pricing, so nothing here is auto-fetched. Each bottle has a "Search on Wine-Searcher" link to check the real current price.
  • Vintage assessments & drinking windows: general, region-level references and rough maturity estimates — a sanity check, not a bottle-specific verdict. Always confirm against a professional vintage chart (Decanter, Wine Spectator, Vinous, or Jancis Robinson).
  • Your account & cellar: stored locally in this browser, not on a server. It's private, but it won't sync across devices and clearing your browser data will erase it.

Want live pricing/scores or real multi-device accounts? Those need a paid data provider and a backend (e.g. Firebase) wired in.
